## ğŸ”„ PR Review & Merge Process

### 1. Initial Review
- At least **2 reviewers** must approve a PR before it can be merged.  
- Automated checks (CI/CD, tests, linters) must pass.  

### 2. Maintainer Approval
- After reviewers approve, a **maintainer** must give the final approval.  

### 3. Merge Policy
- We use **squash merging** to keep a clean commit history.  
- PRs should be up to date with `main` before merging (use rebase if necessary).  
- PRs that do not meet contribution guidelines will be closed with feedback.
